built for surf

Our motto, “Explore More”, says it all. From the beach breaks of the Outer Banks, to the misty forests of Tofino, to spring-fed standing river waves in the Rockies, we are all about the endless search for the perfect wave and crafting the best optics to help you find it. Our entire line of premium-polarized sunglasses are proudly built from plant-based materials to reduce our impacts on our waves, beaches and waterways. Simply stated, life is better #ThroughOurLenses.
